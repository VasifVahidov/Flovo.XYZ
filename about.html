About Flovo AI

Flovo AI is an innovative platform that helps users visualize knowledge in an interactive, structured way. By leveraging a dynamic tree structure, Flovo AI organizes complex topics into digestible steps, making learning easier and more engaging.

Whether you're exploring programming, cloud computing, cybersecurity, books or slides (self-uploaded) or AI, Flovo AI provides an intuitive roadmap for your learning journey.

Our mission is to **make learning feel like an adventure** rather than a struggle. By breaking down complex subjects into bite-sized nodes, Flovo AI ensures that you stay focused, motivated, and in control of your learning journey.
